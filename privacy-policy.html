Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

Quick Summary: Digital Wallet stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

1. Overview

Digital Wallet ("the App") is an iOS application that helps you create and manage digital passes for Apple Wallet. Your privacy is extremely important to us, which is why we've designed the app to keep all your data on your device.

2. Information We Collect

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Voucher details (card names, balances, expiration dates)
  • Card numbers you enter
  • Additional notes you add to cards
  • App preferences (language, appearance settings)
  • Custom card templates you create

3. How We Use Your Information

All information you enter is used exclusively to:

  • Display your saved vouchers and loyalty cards
  • Generate digital passes for Apple Wallet
  • Customize your app experience

4. Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults and local file system)
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data is never synchronized to cloud services
  • No External Servers: We do not operate servers that collect or store user data
  • iOS Backup: Your data may be included in iOS device backups (iCloud or iTunes)

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties. Period.

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No third-party trackers
  • No data brokers

6. Apple Wallet Integration

When you add a pass to Apple Wallet, the pass data is transmitted to Apple's Wallet app on your device. This is a standard iOS function and is necessary for the app to work. We do not control how Apple Wallet stores or manages passes.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We recommend keeping your device secure and using device encryption features provided by iOS.

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your saved vouchers within the app
  • Edit: Modify or update any voucher information
  • Delete: Remove individual vouchers or delete the entire app
  • Export: Generate passes to Apple Wallet at any time

9. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Parents may want to supervise young children's use of the app to ensure they understand how to use it appropriately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the app's data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@wallet-coupons.com

12. Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • iOS data privacy requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
