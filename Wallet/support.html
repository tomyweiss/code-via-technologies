Digital Wallet Support

Welcome to Digital Wallet support! We're here to help you manage your vouchers, refunds, and loyalty cards.

Need help? Contact us:

support@wallet-coupons.com

Features

  • Create digital vouchers and gift cards
  • Manage store refund credits
  • Track loyalty program cards
  • Generate Apple Wallet passes
  • Custom card templates
  • Barcode generation (QR, Code128, PDF417)
  • Local storage - your data stays on your device

Requirements

  • iOS Version: iOS 16.0 or later
  • Device: iPhone or iPad
  • Apple Wallet: Pre-installed on iOS devices

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new voucher?
1. Tap the "+" button on the main screen
2. Select a card template
3. Enter card details (number, balance, expiration)
4. Tap "Save to App" or "Add to Wallet"
Can I edit a saved voucher?
Yes! Tap the pencil icon on any card to edit its details. You can update the balance, expiration date, or any other information.
How do I delete a voucher?
Tap the pencil icon to edit the card, then scroll down and tap "Delete Voucher" at the bottom.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All your voucher data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
What's the difference between "Save to App" and "Add to Wallet"?
Save to App: Stores the voucher in the Digital Wallet app only.
Add to Wallet: Creates a pass and adds it to Apple Wallet for quick access.
Can I create custom cards?
Yes! Select "Custom Voucher" from the card type list, then customize the name, organization, and color to create your own unique card.
Which Israeli retailers are supported?
The app includes templates for popular Israeli retailers including:
  • Tav Plus
  • BuyMe
  • Shufersal
  • Mega
  • Rami Levy
  • And more!
Does this work offline?
Yes! The app works completely offline. All data is stored locally on your device.
Can I export my vouchers?
You can add individual vouchers to Apple Wallet. Currently, bulk export is not supported, but it's on our roadmap for future updates.

Troubleshooting

The app crashes when I open it
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest iOS version
4. If the problem persists, contact support
"Cannot add to Wallet" error
This usually occurs when:
  • Testing on the iOS Simulator (use a real device)
  • Wallet app is restricted in Screen Time settings
  • Device storage is full
My saved vouchers disappeared
If you deleted and reinstalled the app, local data is lost. Unfortunately, we don't have cloud backup (to protect your privacy). Make sure to add important cards to Apple Wallet for backup.
